Fruit Smoothie Recipe

Because you had fruits in your inventory...



Ingredient List:

  1. 2 Cups Strawberries
  2. 1 Banana
  3. 2 Cups Blueberries
  4. 1 Cup Milk
  5. 1/2 Cup Yogurt

Instructions:

  1. Clean the fruit before using.
  2. Insert everything into the blender and blend for 1 minute.
  3. Serve!
